# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Tech recruiters, hiring managers, and prospective clients. They are looking to evaluate technical skills, projects, and work experience to hire a fullstack engineer or sponsor open-source/blog work.

## Product Purpose
To showcase Avinash's expertise as a Fullstack Engineer through a high-craft developer portfolio. Success means presenting projects and experience with clarity, driving blog engagement (reads), and securing professional opportunities.

## Brand Personality
- **Expert & Minimalist**: Clean, understated, and highly focused.
- **Tone**: Technical, precise, and confident.
- **Inspirations**: Vercel and Linear (high-contrast dark mode, typographic center, extreme layout precision).

## Anti-references
- **SaaS/AI Template Slop**: Generic cards with 1px border + soft wide shadow (ghost-cards), side-stripe borders, gradient text, repeating card grids, and uppercase tracked eyebrows on every section.

## Design Principles
- **Typographic Center**: Let type, hierarchy, and content carry the layout rather than decorative shapes.
- **Intentional Contrast**: Use a rich dark background with crisp, legible ink and high-contrast focus states.
- **Rhythmic Layout**: Rely on varied margins, vertical flow, and grid rhythm instead of card wrappers to group and separate information.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Contrast**: Maintain WCAG AA contrast ratios (>= 4.5:1 for body text, >= 3:1 for large text).
- **Navigation**: Support clear focus rings and keyboard-friendly navigation.
- **Motion**: Respect system preferences (`prefers-reduced-motion`) for all visual transitions.
